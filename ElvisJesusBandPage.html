History of the Band

While at school at UNC Chapel Hill, Sam Gray found Tim Leong, an amazing musician with the talent of Freddie Mercury, Buddy Rich, and Jimi Hendrix all mixed together. He asked Tim to join his band to perform at The Library, a local bar. The band's performance was so powerful that it caused 32 attendees to lose their minds. After the show, they were approached by the founder of Interscope Records and Eminem to sign a 12 million dollar contract. This would be the start of the band's journey to play sold out stadiums such as Wembely and Madison Square Garden. Breaking Metallica's record sales in 2017, the band continues to be a dominant force in the music industry.
